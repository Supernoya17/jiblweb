Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex e-commerce sites may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you handle website hosting?

Yes, all our website packages include hosting for the first year. We use reliable, high-performance hosting services to ensure your website loads quickly and remains secure. After the first year, hosting can be renewed at competitive rates.

Can you help with content creation?

Absolutely! Our team includes professional copywriters, photographers, and videographers who can create high-quality content for your website. We tailor content to reflect your brand's voice and connect with your target audience.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and mobile payment methods. For larger projects, we offer installment payment plans. A 50% deposit is typically required to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion.

Do you offer ongoing support after the website launch?

Yes, we offer various maintenance and support packages to keep your website running smoothly. These include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. We recommend a maintenance plan for all websites to ensure they remain secure and up-to-date.
